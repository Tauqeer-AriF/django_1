About Braxton Capital

Driving business performance through investment, advisory, and transformation.

Founded in 2017, Braxton acts as both a "Search Fund" and Advisory business for emerging opportunities throughout Southeast Asia. By deploying capital and advisory capabilities we’re able to help Southeast Asia companies realize significant value creation through transformation.

Being a relatively new company Braxton has spent a significant amount of time evaluating investments looking for unique opportunities in which they can work hand in hand with partner companies to significantly accelerate value creation. This level of diligence allows us to now only properly review investments but also allows Braxton to ensure the right fit when partnering with these growth platforms.

Our Focus

Our focus is on utilizing capital and advisory expertise to accelerate value creation for mid market businesses in Southeast Asia. In particular, Braxton looks at business acquisitions and investments, leveraging strategic advice, and accelerating the transformation agenda. We work hand in hand w investors and investee companies to leverage their embedded capabilities while creating a step change in growth.

Search Fund

We operate as a "Search Fund" where we identify opportunities, fund and structure transactions, and then work directly with the investee company to enhance their businesses. To date, we’ve evaluated several opportunities for potential search fund investments leading to several transactions.

Advisory

We bring a significant amount of insight based on a history in Southeast Asia focused on Financial Services, Insurance, and Healthcare across multiple disciplines (Strategy, Investments, M&A, and Transformation). Over the past several years we’ve worked with a handful of the leading management consultancies in a senior advisory role as well as at the Board level for investee companies.
